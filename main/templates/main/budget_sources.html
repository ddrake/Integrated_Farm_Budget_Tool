Yield and Expense Budget Sources

Last modified: October 11, 2023

The "Yield and Expense Budgets" available for selection at the bottom of the "Crop Acreage/Crop Insurance" page are derived from publicly-available budgets published by a number of universities in the U.S.  We're grateful to the faculty and staff of these universities for their efforts in assembling budgets from contributors in their states, and hope that the inclusion of yield and expense budgets in this application is beneficial to our users.

In most cases, in order to display a standard set of cost items, it's necessary to re-categorize the some of the cost items of the original university budget. Also, note that the budget data used in 'Yield and Expense Budgets' is not necessarily derived from the latest university budget released for the crop year.
